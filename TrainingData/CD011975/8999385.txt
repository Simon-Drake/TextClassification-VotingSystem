[Ultrasonographic diagnosis of cystic hygroma at the 15th week. A case report].
The authors present a case of cystic hygroma arrived to their observation at the 15th week. In succession to the precocious ultrasonographic screening of the malformation, have performed that the chromosome map have revealed that the cause is the trisomy 21.
