[Diagnosis of a trisomy 21 in the first pregnancy trimester. Value of trophoblast sampling].
The authors report a diagnosis of trisomy 21 in the 11th week following the last menstrual period in a patient of 40 years of age. This was made by chorion villi aspiration and direct cytogenetic examination.
